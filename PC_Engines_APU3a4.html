PC Engines APU3a4

Date ven. 21 juillet 2017 Modified ven. 28 juillet 2017 Tags Hardware / APU

PC Engines offers small and low power computer that is perfectly suitable for a personal server or firewall. It's a good alternative to Soekris that is not really commercialized in European countries. This post describes the hardware configuration of my APU3a4. Future articles will refer to this one.

Serial console

A PC Engines doesn't have graphical processor unit. The only way to interact with this computer is a serial console with a null-modem cable (DB9 female). On top of that, if you don't have a serial port on your laptop, an USB serial converter is needed. Calexium provides the "QinHeng Electronics HL-340 USB-Serial adapter".
