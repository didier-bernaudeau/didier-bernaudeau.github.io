Extracting video from youtube

Date sam. 15 septembre 2018 Modified sam. 15 septembre 2018 Tags Python / video / music

This post describes a simple way to extract video or sound from YouTube with "youtube-dl", a useful tool in python.

  • List all available formats of requested videos
$ youtube-dl --list-formats https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=XXXXXXXXXXX

[youtube] XXXXXXXXXXX: Downloading webpage
[youtube] XXXXXXXXXXX: Downloading video info webpage
[youtube] XXXXXXXXXXX: Extracting video information
[info] Available formats for XXXXXXXXXXX:
format code  extension  resolution note

160          mp4        256x144    144p  106k , avc1.4d400c, 25fps, video only, 1.60MiB
133          mp4        426x240    240p  191k , avc1.4d4015, 25fps, video only, 2.95MiB
134          mp4        640x360    360p  401k , avc1.4d401e, 25fps, video only, 5.79MiB
135          mp4        854x480    480p  671k , avc1.4d401e, 25fps, video only, 9.07MiB
136          mp4        1280x720   720p 1063k , avc1.4d401f, 25fps, video only, 13.60MiB
137          mp4        1920x1080  1080p 1597k , avc1.640028, 25fps, video only, 20.25MiB
18           mp4        640x360    medium , avc1.42001E, mp4a.40.2@ 96k (best)
  • Download
$ youtube-dl --format FormatCode https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=XXXXXXXXXXX
  • Extract audio from video
$ ffmpeg -i video.mp4 -b:a 192K -vn audio.mp3

The -vn options is used to skip inclusion of video.
